
# Tic-Tac-Toe
2 Players vs AI modes — toggle between playing a friend or battling a perfect Minimax AI (unbeatable!)
Live scoreboard — tracks wins for X, O, and draws across multiple rounds
Animated marks — X and O draw themselves with a smooth stroke animation
Win detection — winning cells glow and a line is drawn across the winning combo
Turn indicator — score cards highlight whose turn it is
AI thinking animation — pulsing dots while the AI calculates its move
Dark, premium aesthetic — deep navy background, red/cyan accent pairing, Bebas Neue display font
